How to use the platform
Getting Started
  1. Click New Campaign and complete the 5-step wizard
  2. Copy the Field App link and share with enumerators via WhatsApp
  3. Send each enumerator an invite link from the Operations monitor
  4. Monitor responses live from the Analytics Dashboard
  5. Close the survey when fieldwork is complete
